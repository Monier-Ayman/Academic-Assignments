Meet Jack

This is Jack. He's an odd little boy who just got an empty box for his birthday. He may look happy, but he's a little disappointed. Now he'll have to put a few things in the box.

Jack would really like...

  • Roller blades
  • Magic tricks
  • Anything Pokemon
  • A motorized Lego set

You can get all this (and more) at an online toy store. Two very popular ones are toysrus.com and etoys.com.
